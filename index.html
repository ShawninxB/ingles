The Challenge of Defining 'Fairness' in AI

Posted by: DataEthicist On: Oct 26, 2024
It's difficult to implement fairness metrics when there isn't a universal definition. Should we aim for equal opportunity, equal outcome, or something else entirely? Different contexts require different approaches, leading to constant trade-offs.

Case Study: Bias in Facial Recognition Systems

Posted by: PrivacyAdvocate On: Oct 25, 2024
Studies show significant accuracy disparities, often performing worse on darker-skinned individuals, particularly women. This is a clear case of dataset bias that needs urgent regulatory intervention.

About This Forum

This forum was created to foster meaningful discussions about one of the most pressing issues in artificial intelligence today: bias and discrimination.

Our Mission

To create a space where developers, researchers, ethicists, and concerned citizens can discuss, analyze, and propose solutions to address bias in AI systems.
